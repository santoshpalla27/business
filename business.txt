Personal Gifts

Bought by individuals
Emotional value > branding
Customization is critical (names, photos, dates)
Lower quantity, higher per-unit personalization

Examples

Birthday gifts
Wedding favors
Anniversary items
Personalized home décor


Corporate Gifts

Bought by companies
Branding, durability, professionalism matter
Medium to large quantity
Consistency + repeat orders = stable revenue

Examples :-
Employee onboarding kits
Client appreciation gifts
Event & conference merchandise
Festive corporate hampers

Technologies:- 

1. Sublimation 
2. laser cutting
3. laser engraving 
4. metal marking
5. uv printing

Technology Comparison (Business View)

| Technology      | Best For            | Client Type        | Margin      |
| --------------- | ------------------- | ------------------ | ----------- |
| Sublimation     | Photo gifts         | Individuals + SMEs | Medium–High |
| Laser Cutting   | Décor & shapes      | Events, designers  | High        |
| Laser Engraving | Premium gifts       | Corporate HR       | High        |
| Metal Marking   | Industrial branding | Enterprises        | Very High   |
| UV Printing     | Premium branding    | Agencies           | High        |
