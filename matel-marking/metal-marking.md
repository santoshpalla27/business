# Metal Marking (Fiber Laser)

## What it is
Permanent laser marking on metal (steel, aluminum, brass).

## Business Strength
- Industrial-grade permanence
- Cannot be scratched or washed away
- Compliance-friendly

## Products You Can Sell

### Personal Gifts
- Metal wallets
- Stainless steel bottles
- Bracelets
- Keychains

### Corporate Gifts
- Premium bottles
- Tools
- Laptop plates
- Industrial parts with serial numbers

## Why businesses buy it

- Legal compliance (serials, QR codes)
- Premium durability
- Strong brand reputation
