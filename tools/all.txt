              Raster vs Vector (CRITICAL CONCEPT)

Raster (Photoshop)

Made of pixels
Quality depends on resolution
Enlarging causes blur / pixelation
Examples: Photos, scanned images, textures

Vector (Illustrator)

Made of paths & mathematical shapes
Infinitely scalable
No quality loss at any size

Examples: Logos, icons, illustrations   


Examples: Logos, icons, illustrations
