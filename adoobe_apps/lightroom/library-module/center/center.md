# CENTER AREA — VIEW MODES

## 1. Grid View (G)

Shows thumbnails.

Used for:

- Culling
- Rating
- Comparing

### Shortcut: G

## 2. Loupe View (E)

Single image large preview.  open a single image ulike grid view where we see multiple 

Used for:

- Detailed inspection
- Checking focus
- Reviewing selection

### Shortcut: E

## 3. Compare View (C)

Compare 2 images side by side.

Used for:
Selecting best expression in portraits.

## 4. Survey View (N)

Compare multiple images.

### Best for:
Group photos → picking best face.
