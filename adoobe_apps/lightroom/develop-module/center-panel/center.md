# CENTER AREA (Workspace)

This is where you view and inspect the image.

## A. Main Image Window

Shows full resolution preview.

## B. Toolbar (Below Image)

### Shortcut: T to show/hide

### Contains:

- Crop Overlay
- Spot Removal
- Red Eye Correction
- Masking (new AI masks)
- Comparison View

## C. Zoom Controls

### Click image or press:

- Spacebar → Zoom 100%
- Z → Toggle zoom

### Professional Rule:

- Always check sharpening & skin at 100%.


## D. Before / After View

### Shortcut:

- \ → Quick toggle   ( best for comparion  )
- Y → Side-by-side

# Professional habit:

- Constantly compare before/after to avoid over-editing.

## CENTER AREA MINDSET

Editing psychology:
If you don’t zoom → you miss mistakes.
If you don’t compare → you over-edit.
