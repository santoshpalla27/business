## 1. Inkjet Printing (Liquid Ink Printing)

Inkjet printing uses liquid ink sprayed in microscopic droplets onto paper or media through a printhead.

**Ink type**

- Dye ink (bright colors, photo quality)
- Pigment ink (water-resistant, longer life)
- Sublimation ink (for heat transfer printing)

**How it works**

- Digital image sent to printer
- Printhead sprays tiny ink droplets
- Ink absorbs into or coats the media surface
- Ink dries naturally (air or heat-assisted)
